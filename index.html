“Hold No More” has a very simple mission – to demand that companies offer something better than waiting on hold to get help.

Research suggests that Americans lose 108 billion dollars a year in productivity by wasting time on hold. Let’s make noise and demand a better standard for customer service.

Join the #HoldNoMore movement and help us end the pain of waiting on hold. If you’d like to get involved, take to social media with the #HoldNoMore hashtag whenever you find yourself waiting on hold for customer service. Let them know you’ll #HoldNoMore!
